Welcome to Eco-Atlanta, your home for news, guides, and a community geared towards all things green and sustainable in Atlanta! The purpose of this Eco-Atlanta.com is to inform the Atlanta community about ways to protect the environment, promote sustainable living within the community, and to provide a central location for citizens interested in environmental issues to communicate with one another, locate relevant information, and to facilitate collaboration to start new projects.




The site is still under construction, so please pardon any bugs as well as the minimal content. If you would like to make a recommendation for our site, please head over to the "Submit a Ticket" page under the Support tab and let us know!

We hope that you enjoy our site and that we can make you more informed of how to keep our beautiful city clean, green, and sustainable!

Thank you for visiting.
