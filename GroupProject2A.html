Eco-Atlanta.com


ORGANIZATION DESCRIPTION:


The purpose of this Eco-Atlanta.com is to inform the Atlanta community about ways to protect the environment, promote sustainable living within the community, and to provide a central location for citizens interested in environmental issues to communicate with one another, locate relevant information, and to facilitate collaboration to start new projects.



GOALS:


The main goal of the Eco-Atlanta.com is to provide local environmental organizations a general forum to assist with organizing events, providing information about recycling or eco-friendly living, and to store a list of environmentally friendly products and services.



BUSINESS OBJECTIVE:


Eco-Atlanta.com’s objective is to facilitate the creation of ecologically friendly projects, to provide support for the community, and to disseminate information as needed.



SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED BY THE ORGANIZATION:


Eco-Atlanta intends to provide information such as how-to articles for sustainable environmental practices, guides to recycling in different areas of the city, informational graphics concerning useful environmental practices, video guides, news about environmental projects, and information on Atlanta’s public transit and the beltline. The event planning portion of the site will provide a calendar so users can easily plan activities, meetings, volunteer opportunities, and workshops in the area, as well as to provide sign-up opportunities. The Organizations portion of the site will provide links and descriptions for various Atlanta based organizations and green living communities. The Forum’s purpose is to allow for information to be easily shared between visitors to the site and to encourage discussions among the community. Finally, there will be a Vacations section of Eco-Atlanta.com where information will be available to support economically friendly vacations here in the city.



PROJECTED COMMUNITY:


The projected community is Atlanta citizens and tourists to the area. Anyone interested in the environment and “green” living is encouraged to visit and contribute to the website.



GENERAL DEMOGRAPHICS
Age Group

Eco-Atlanta will be more focused on adults, though there will be no restrictions on forum based content for individuals to share with their children or to take to their schools.
Sex

Both genders are expected and encouraged to use Eco-Atlanta.com.
Nationality

There is no requirement or expectation of any specific national origin to use the site. Discrimination based on this or any factor will not be tolerated.


User Categories
User:

These individuals are those who use the website to fulfill its intended functions. Users can post in the forums, send messages to other users, sign up for events, and submit help tickets.
Eco-Staff:

These users are allowed the permissions of a standard user as well as the ability to create and edit events or projects.
Administrator:

The administrators are the website’s normal operation staff. They have the ability to edit users, edit forum posts of other users, unlock user accounts, lock forums, as well as sharing the rest of the permissions of the Eco-Staff.


Website Use Requirements
Education Level:

Anyone interested in the environment is encouraged to visit the website. They must be able to read.

Computer Literacy

Computer literacy is a must. The user must be able to navigate an internet browser.

Language Requirement

English speakers, and the website will be written in English.
Expected Interests

Environmental change, sustainability, “green” living, public transit


Tasks & Actions
Task Action
Learn About Eco-Atlanta

Users will be able to view the FAQ at any time in order to familiarize themselves with Eco-Atlanta in order to provide a better user experience.
Register

Site registration will possible in order to allow users to participate and provide a means for the community to grow.
Browse and search the forums

Users will be able to browse the forums regularly in order to learn more about living environmentally friendly, as well as to search for specific posts when desired.

Create Forum topics

Forum topic creation will be possible by any level of user, allowing for information to be shared freely by any who want to read it.
Join A Group

Users will be able to join groups in order to help them connect with their neighbors and more localized areas of the city in an easier fashion.
Event Sign-Up

Signing up for an event will be done through the calendar or event list,, allowing for event planners to easily manage the expected turnout and scope of the projects.
Event Creation

Events will be created by Eco-Staff in order to help the community organize itself more easily.
Submit Trouble Ticket

Trouble tickets may be submitted at any time should a user run into complications that prevent them from fully utilizing Eco-Atlanta.com
