Eco-Atlanta High Level Design


Conceptual Design


The Eco-Atlanta website will use CSS stylesheets to maintain a coherent, uniform appearance. The site will utilize a database for user information and forum content. All pages will feature a navigation sidebar with hyperlink buttons allowing easy access to all areas of the site from any other part of the site.



Media Types

The Eco-Atlanta site will use images with file formats .gif, .jpg, or .png, which may be uploaded by users or site administration. There may be embedded video on various parts of the site which will most likely be embedded from YouTube or another web source, but may be uploaded by site administration if original content is needed.


Individual Page Details

Homepage

The Homepage of the Eco-Atlanta website will display the Eco-Atlanta logo as well as images of/links to upcoming important events, breaking news, or other site material deemed especially useful or interesting by site administrators. There will be text, images, and potentially video.

Register

The Registration sub-page will allow users to create an account with a username and password. This account will allow users to participate in the forum and group features of Eco-Atlanta. The Registration page will consist of text and a form users may fill out and submit to become members.

Forum

The Forum sub-page will allow registered users to begin discussions on topics of their choice and contribute to discussions with other users. The Forum pages will be constructed with PHP and MySQL, and will consist primarily of text but allow users to upload images as well.

Groups

The Groups sub-page will allow registered users to form a Facebook-style group for specific interests within the scope of environmental activism. For example, there may be a group for users that want to be active in trash-pickup days around the city in particular. Groups may involve group-specific forums, newsletters, and contact information availability for other group members. The Group page will use text, images, and possibly videos - similar to the Forum page.

Learn

The Learn sub-pages will consist of news, articles, how-to guides, and infographics related to Eco-Atlanta's mission of spreading environmental awareness in the Atlanta area. This content will include text, images, and embedded video created or selected by site administration.

Events

The Events sub-page will have a monthly calendar so that users can see upcoming environmental-related events, such as volunteer opportunities, workshops, and meetings. Registered users will be able to sign up for events that require advance registration. The Events sub-page will use text and images.

Links

The Links portion of the site will be a directory of local organizations that are related to the goals of Eco-Atlanta. There will be an alphabetical listing of businesses, nonprofits, and other groups that are providing eco-friendly services or opportunities to be involved in the environmental action community. Users may view short descriptions of each organization and access a link to each organization's website.

Visiting Atlanta

The Visiting Atlanta area of the site will provide reference information for visitors to the Atlanta area who wish to minimize their environmental impact while enjoying their stay. There will be links to eco-friendly businesses and information about environmentally sound activities in the area. There will be text, images, and potentially video in this area of the site.

Help

The Help section will involve two subcategories, a FAQ and a trouble-ticket submission area. The FAQ will have generalized solutions to problems or questions users may have regarding the site or related material, and the trouble-ticket area will allow users to submit feedback forms regarding site issues. The Help pages will use text and images.
